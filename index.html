The race weekend

The Abu Dhabi GP runs Friday 4 to Sunday 6 December at Yas Marina, the season finale. Your grandstand ticket includes the Yasalam after race concerts at Etihad Park, and the 2026 bill is the strongest of any Grand Prix: Metallica, Katy Perry, Post Malone, Imagine Dragons and Lewis Capaldi, with an Afterlife after party for the late nights. Basing in Dubai keeps you out of the inflated on circuit Yas Island hotels and gives you the food and nightlife scene for the rest of the week.

Where to eat

  • Trèsind Studio — three Michelin stars, a storytelling tasting menu reinventing Indian cuisine. The blow out night; book well ahead.
  • Row on 45 — Jason Atherton's twelve seat counter, seventeen courses in three acts.
  • Pierchic — seafood on a pier over the Gulf with the Burj Al Arab in view, the classic Dubai dinner.
  • Long Teng — a genuine hidden gem in Business Bay for fresh seafood Chinese and dim sum.
  • Sikka Cafe — Emirati coffee and luqaimat in the atmospheric alleys of old Al Fahidi for a relaxed lunch.
